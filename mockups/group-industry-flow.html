Distribution Formula

Payout = Costs Engaged x (1 + Margin%)

= Costs Engaged x (Net Revenue / Total Project Cost)

Example: Alice contributed 2.20B out of 4.20B total cost

Payout = 2.20B x (1 + 29.3%) = 2.20B x 1.293 = 2.85B

LOSS

Loss Scenario

If the project results in a loss (negative margin), each contributor absorbs their proportional share. For example, with a -10% margin on 4.20B costs: Alice (52.4%) would receive 2.20B x 0.90 = 1.98B (a loss of 220M on her 2.20B contribution). The formula remains the same, but (1 + margin%) becomes less than 1.
